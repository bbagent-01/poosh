Concept spec · for the jeweler

Custom Gold Bead Necklace

Three engraved birth-chart beads — sun · moon · rising — on a fine gold chain.

01

The necklace

A very fine gold chain threaded through the centre hole of three small beads, side by side. The chain is shown for context only — it is not part of the commission.

02

The bead

Each bead is a tiny gold hex-nut shape with softened, rounded corners and edges — pleasant to touch, no sharp points. Six flat facets around the outside; a hole through the centre for the chain.

Top view

As small as is practical: about 3.5–4 mm across the flats, ~2.5–3 mm tall, hole under 1 mm — final call with the jeweler. The limit on going smaller is engraving legibility (six tiny icons per bead), not the gold. Engraving this fine wants laser engraving.

03

The engraving — six facets per bead

Reading around each bead: a symbol for the sun, then the person's sun sign; the moon, then their moon sign; rising, then their rising sign. Same pattern on all three beads — only the signs change. (Symbol style still to choose — see section 04.)
